WEZTERM — PANE MANAGEMENT
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Leader: Ctrl+Space — tap it, release, then press the next key within 1.5s.
  Comma below means "then":  Ctrl+Space, h  =  leader, then h.

SPLIT — LOCAL
  Ctrl+Space, h      split down         new pane below (stacked, top/bottom)
  Ctrl+Space, v      split right        new pane on the right (side-by-side)

SPLIT — INTO A DOMAIN                   Shift = "remote": fuzzy-pick a domain
  Ctrl+Space, H      → down             (local, WSL, SSH:* …) from a list, then
  Ctrl+Space, V      → right            split that domain in (below / right)

NAVIGATE
  Ctrl+Space, j      left
  Ctrl+Space, k      right
  Ctrl+Space, i      up
  Ctrl+Space, m      down

RESIZE                                  uppercase — hold Shift with the key
  Ctrl+Space, J      expand left        5-unit steps; repeat to taste
  Ctrl+Space, K      expand right
  Ctrl+Space, I      expand up
  Ctrl+Space, M      expand down

ZOOM, POP & CLOSE
  Ctrl+Space, z      toggle zoom        fills the window; again to restore
  Ctrl+Space, o      pop to window      moves pane into its own WezTerm window
  Ctrl+Shift+O       pop to window      quick-access alias (no leader)
  Ctrl+Space, x      close pane         closes this pane (confirms first)

GRID — 3 ACROSS × 2 DOWN                press to build that cell, or focus it if
  F1  F2  F3   top row                  it exists; also Ctrl+Space, 1-6.
  F4  F5  F6   bottom row               Shift+F# opens the new cell in a domain.
  Columns fill to even thirds, rows to even halves, as you build the grid.
